## 12. Backlit Elegant Back Editorial（美背 / 逆光 / 露背）

當使用者要求「美背」「背影」「露背」「逆光」「逆光美背」「背部線條」時使用。

**核心方向**：
- tasteful open-back fashion design
- backlit elegant back silhouette
- refined shoulder and neck line
- subtle shoulder blade contour, spine center line hint, natural waist-back curve
- soft fabric layers with refined textures
- cinematic light and shadow

**推薦服裝與材質**：
- tasteful open-back evening dress
- elegant low-back knit dress
- silk shawl over the shoulders
- soft cotton-linen dress with an open-back cut
- 細閃紗 / 真絲 / 薄紗 / 軟垂感長裙
- **布料參與構圖**：`graceful fabric drapery participating in the composition...`

**姿態**：
- standing near a window with her back partly toward the camera
- gentle over-the-shoulder glance
- one hand softly arranging her hair
- natural weight shift, composed adult posture

**光線設定**：
- soft window backlight outlining shoulder, neck, and spine ridge
- side backlight that catches hair strands and fabric edge

## 16. Elegant Neckline and Collar Editorial（領口 / 頸部 / 鎖骨）

**核心方向**：
- tasteful and high-fashion neckline or collar design
- elegant neck, shoulder, and collarbone contour
- soft fabric texture, natural drape, and structural lines

**推薦服裝**：
- cowl-neck silk top or dress
- boat-neck linen blouse
- classic V-neck cashmere sweater
- sophisticated high-collar minimal cotton shirt

**光線設定**：
- soft directional studio light or natural side window light highlighting the neck and collarbone contour

**避免**：
- low-angle body-focused shot focusing on chest gap
- deep cleavage focus

---

## 17. 視覺媒材模式（Photorealistic vs 3D CG）

**預設**：photorealistic editorial portrait

**切換條件**：使用者明確要求「3D CG / 二次元 / 角色渲染 / 幻想系角色 / anime style」時，才切換。

**Photorealistic 模式禁止混入**：anime / 3D CG / subsurface scattering / illustration style

**3D CG 模式必須包含**：
- high-end 3D CG character render
- subsurface scattering
- soft global illumination
- refined fabric simulation
- detailed hair strands

**兩種模式都必須避免**：childlike sexualization、explicit nudity、teen-coded styling。
